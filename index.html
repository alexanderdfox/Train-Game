EPILEPSY WARNING

Photosensitive Seizure Warning

This game contains flashing lights, rapid color changes, and animated effects that may trigger seizures in people with photosensitive epilepsy or other photosensitive conditions.

  • Rapidly flashing or strobing lights
  • Fast-moving animations and transitions
  • Rapid color changes and visual effects
  • Bright, contrasting visual elements

If you or anyone in your household has a history of epilepsy or seizures, please consult a doctor before playing.
